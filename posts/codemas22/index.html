THE 12 PROJECTS OF CODEMAS #

I have spent the first 12 days of Christmas making a project a day with the new(ish) Maker Calender. It has been an absolute blast. It includes a gide for each day that will coach you through it. It seems quite expensive but for the amount of things you get it is quite cheap. You can buy it here. It is a nice piece of kit for a bit of fun over the holidays. Let's just say if there is one next year it's in my shopping basket. It is also good for expanding you parts selection with new and wacky pieces of kit.
